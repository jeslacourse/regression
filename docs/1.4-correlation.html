1.4 Correlation

(!) Correlations is not equal to causation in non-random studies

Correlation is defined on the scale \(-1 \leq corr(X,Y) \leq 1\) where:

\[corr(X,Y) = \frac{cov(X,Y)}{\sqrt{var(X)var(Y)}}\]
